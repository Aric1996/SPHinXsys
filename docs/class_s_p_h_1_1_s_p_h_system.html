Default constructor. Note that the lower and upper domian bounds are used to build mesh cell linked list, therefore, should be bigger than the lower and upper bounds of all bodies plus the boundary width. Otherwise, it may come to the situation that a particle may can not found a valid cell to build the cell linked list.
